SplitStat Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs the "SplitStat Tracker" Discord bot.
It will go over what is stored, for how long, and where it's stored.

SplitStat operates the SplitStat Discord Bot & all content at https://awexxx.xyz/splitstatbot.
SplitStat will never ask for any personal information unless we so need it to provide the Service.
SplitStat will only log/retain data that may be collected from you adding the bot to the server, any commands that were ran and by who, and specific info regarding your stats.. This includes but is not limited to;

  • Guild IDs
  • Timestamps of join/leave
  • Guild Owner usernames and discriminators
  • Certain Game Stat Data served by Tracker Network's Application Programming Interface (API)

Game Data/Usernames is/are not permanently stored and/or logged in any place other than the Discord channel you request it in, it is only cached for 15 minutes and then discarded to make your user experience better.
Game Data/Usernames that is/are temporarily stored includes but is not limited to:
  • Various Player Stats (K/D, wins, losses, etc)
  • Recent Match Data/IDs
  • Various Splitgate/Tracker Network Profile Data

This data is only cached once you call for it.
If you run into an error, SplitStat may generate a "case ID" and assign it to your username and disclose it with the Developer. All of this is private and is never shared with the public.
This policy only affects SplitStat. SplitStat may link to external sources that have different terms & privacy notices, and SplitStat does not have control over these sites/what they do with your information.
