Projects

What projects have you worked on, you ask, well I'm here to answer that question! (there's a bit omg)

Medal Clip Webhook

This is a simple web app that POSTs to a Discord webhook.
It allows users outside of the Medal.tv platform to allow their users to share their clips to Discord without the app (ex. for Mac users).

mcsrvstat-wrapper

This is a simple Node.js wrapper that allows users to easily fetch the status of their (or someone elses doesn't matter) Minecraft server via the MCSRVSTAT API.

medaltv-wrapper

This is yet another Node.js (soon web) wrapper that allows you to easily fetch Medal clips from the Medal.tv REST API.
