Who is Juice WRLD?

Jarad Anthony Higgins was a rapper, singer and songwriter from Chicago. He struck stardom with his hit songs "Lucid Dreams" and "All Girls Are The Same" in 2018.
Between 2018 and 2019, he released 2 albums; "Goodbye & Good Riddance" & "Death Race for Love" and recorded thousands of songs.
Unfortunately, on December 8th 2019, Jarad suffered a codeine and oxycodone overdose after landing at Chicago International Airport.
