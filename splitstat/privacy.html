Intro

The SplitStat Bot is a Discord bot developed by Alex. This bot is not affiliated with Tracker.gg nor Splitgate/1047 Games.
Throughout this document, The SplitStat Bot will be referred to by the terms "we, our, us, the Service" or "SplitStat Bot".

What data is collected?

Below is a list of data we collect, why we collect it, and what is done with it.

  • IGNs (In-Game Names) - Used to find your player profile via Tracker.gg's API. Only used to make a web request, never stored on the Service's end.
  • Player Platforms - Used to find your player profile via Tracker.gg's API. Only used to make a web request, never stored on the Service's end.
  • Discord Display Name (username#1234) - Logged in errors & used to identify misuse of the Service
  • Discord Guild Name - Logged in errors & used to identify misuse of the Service
All data is collected within reason and can be withdrawn anytime by ceasing use of the Service.

How is the data used?

For select items (IGNs, Player Platforms), this is not stored locally and immediately used to request user profiles from Tracker.gg.
For Discord-provided items, like your username and guild, these are logged to the Service in order to prevent misuse of the Service and properly handle issues that may arise.
SplitStat does not disclose this information with any third-party platform unless required by law.

Can I refuse data collection?

You are free to refuse our data collection, however understand that the Service may not be able to provide some services.
